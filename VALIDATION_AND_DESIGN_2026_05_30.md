## ¬ß7 THE CONFIRMED DESIGN ‚Äî "Contract Boundaries Fail Loud"
**Principle:** every structural contract (valid stage names; valid keys per stage; fingerprint-excluded keys) has exactly ONE owner. Consumers DERIVE from it (`dataclasses.fields()`) or VERIFY against it at a boundary that FAILS LOUD (raise) or SURFACES (warn) on drift. Swallowing boundaries that hide a contract mismatch are eliminated/narrowed. Serves precise/traceable/monitorable experiments: every drift that today corrupts/drops/silently-no-ops becomes loud or visible.
